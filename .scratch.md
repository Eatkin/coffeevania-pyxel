Intended flow of game:
- You can go left, right at the start -> left impassable obstacle
- right you drop down
- - Go left -> impassable obstacle
- - Go right -> Free coffee which demonstrates the time dilation mechanic
- Go back left -> chill platforming, coffee, unlocks 'shortcut'
- Go back to top -> you can 'jump rope' over the saw now and make it to the other end
- Chill platforming maybe we leap over the top of the level and ascend the big mountain you see at the beginning and drop down
- We can drop down into the room we see at the beginning by the first coffee, which is now easily navigable
- Finally we can bypass that chaotic saw that protects a coffee -> replace coffee with a hole
- We can then have a linear run to exit as the game turns into bullet hell, but each coffee slows everything, so the game becomes easier as patterns become more chaotic
- We find the world's ultimate cup of coffee and ascend to coffee nirvana
